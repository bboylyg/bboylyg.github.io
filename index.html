Yige Li

I'm a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Singapore Management University in Singapore. I work at the School of Computing and Information Systems, supervised by Prof. Jun Sun, and I also work closely with Prof. Xingjun Ma at Fudan University.

I completed my Ph.D. at Xidian University under the supervision of Prof. Xixiang Lyu. My research focuses on Trustworthy AI and AI Safety, especially backdoor attacks and defenses, large language model security, and agentic AI systems.

Research

My research lies at the intersection of Trustworthy AI, AI Safety, and large-model security. I am particularly interested in backdoor attacks and defenses, controllable and aligned behaviors in large language models, and security risks in agentic AI systems.
